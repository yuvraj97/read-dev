Slutsky's Theorem

Assume we have two sequences of random variables \(T_n\) and \(U_n\) and assume:
\(T_n \xrightarrow [n\to \infty]{(d)} T\quad\) and \(\quad\) \(U_n \xrightarrow [n\to \infty ]{\mathbb{P}} u\)
Where \(T\) is a r.v. and \(u\) is a deterministic number \( \mathbb{P}(U=u)=1 \) \[T_n + U_n \xrightarrow [n\to \infty ]{(d)} T + u\] \[T_n U_n \xrightarrow [n\to \infty ]{(d)} Tu\] \[\displaystyle {\frac{T_n}{U_n} \xrightarrow [n\to \infty ]{(d)} \frac{T}{u}};\text{if u} \neq 0\]
