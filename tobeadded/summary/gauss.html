Say we have \(n\) \(i.i.d\) observations \(X_1,X_2,X_3,....,X_n\sim\mathcal{N}(\mu,\sigma^2)\), \(\mathbb{E}[X]=\mu\) and \(Var(X)=\sigma^2\)

PDF of \(\mathcal{N}(0,1)\) \[f(x)=\frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{2\pi }} \exp \left(-\frac{(x-\mu )^2}{2 \sigma ^2}\right)\] Standardization(Normalization) \[\frac{X-\mu}{\sigma}\sim\mathcal{N}(0,1)\] Quantile of order \(1-\alpha\) denoted as \(q_{\alpha}\) \[F(q_\alpha)=\mathbb{P}(X \leq q_\alpha)=1-\alpha\]
