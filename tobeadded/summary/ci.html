Asymptotic Confidence Interval

Asymptotic Confidence Interval (C.I.) for \(\theta\) of level \( 1-\alpha \) is any random interval \(\mathcal{I}\), it depends on our data \((X_1,\cdots,X_n)\) and it do not depends on \(\theta\) (which is unknown)
\[\lim_{n\to\infty}\mathbb{P}_\theta[\theta\in\mathcal{I}]\geq 1-\alpha\] Say that estimator of \(\theta\) is \(\hat{\theta}_n\)
And \(\hat{\theta}_n \xrightarrow [n\to \infty ]{\mathbb{P},\text{ a.s.}} \theta\) \[\lim_{n\to\infty}\mathbb{P}\left( \theta\in \left[ \hat{\theta}_n - q_{\frac{\alpha}{2}} \sqrt{\text{Var}(\hat{\theta}_n)}, \hat{\theta}_n + q_{\frac{\alpha}{2}} \sqrt{\text{Var}(\hat{\theta}_n)} \right]\right) = 1-\alpha\]
