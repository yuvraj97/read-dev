Goal
Goal of this guide is to help you understand the Fundamental concepts in Statistics.
  • First we will see the underlying (theoretical) idea behind the concept.
  • Then we will visualize that concept (at https://app.quantml.org/stats/ ).
  • Then we will use programing language  ,     to fill the holes and fully grasp that concept.
