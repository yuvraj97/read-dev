Total Variation Distance tells us how far is our estimated probability distribution from the true probability distribution.
Say we have a sample of \(n\) i.i.d. r.v. \(X_1, X_2 \cdots X_n\), and say our Statistical Model is \(\left(E,(\mathbb{P_\theta})_{\theta\in\Theta}\right)\)
\(E\): Sample space
\((\mathbb{P_\theta})_{\theta\in\Theta}\): it's family of probability distribution on \(E\)
\(\Theta\): Parameter set
Assume that \(\theta^*\) is our true parameter, so \(X_i\sim \mathbb{P}_{\theta^*}\).

Goal

Given our sample \(X_1, X_2 \cdots X_n\) we want to find an estimator \(\hat{\theta}\) such that \(\mathbb{P}_{\hat{\theta}}\) is close to \(\mathbb{P}_{\theta^*}\), where \(\theta^*\) is our true parameter.

But what does it means for two probability distribution to be close?
It means that when we compute the probability of an event (say \(A\)), then both probability distribution should give close result.
So we want \(| \mathbb{P}_{\hat{\theta}}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta^*}(A)|\) to be small for all \(A\subset E\)
So the Total Variation Distance between two probability measures \(\mathbb{P}_{\theta}\) and \(\mathbb{P}_{\theta'}\) is defined by \[ \color{blue}{\boxed{ \begin{matrix} \\ %blank line \quad % equation TV\left(\mathbb{P}_{\theta}, \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}\right)= \max_{A\subset E}|\mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta^*}(A)| %equation \quad\\ \\ %blank line \end{matrix} }} \]
So if \(TV\left(\mathbb{P}_{\theta}, \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}\right)=0\) it means that maximum possible deviation of \(\mathbb{P}_{\theta}\) and \(\mathbb{P}_{\theta'}\) is \(0\).
So there is no deviation between \(\mathbb{P}_{\theta}\) and \(\mathbb{P}_{\theta'}\) it means that \(\mathbb{P}_{\theta}\) and \(\mathbb{P}_{\theta'}\) are same distribution.

Claim

\[ \color{green}{|\mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta^*}(A)| = \frac{1}{2}\sum_{x\in E}|\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x) |}\]
\(\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) = \mathbb{P}_{\theta}(X=x)\) and \(\mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x) = \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(X=x)\)
Proof: Say that \(A=\{x\in E: \mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \gt \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)\}\)
\[\mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A) = \sum_{x:\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \gt \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)} \underbrace{(\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x))}_{\gt 0} \] \[\Rightarrow \mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A) = \sum_{x:\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \gt \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)} |\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)| \] \[\Rightarrow \color{red}{|\mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A)| = \sum_{x:\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \gt \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)} |\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)|} \tag{1} \] We have \(A=\{x\in E: \mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \gt \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)\}\), so \(A^c = E-A\).
So,
\[\mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A^c) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A^c) = \sum_{x:\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \leq \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)} \underbrace{(\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x))}_{\leq 0} \] \[\Rightarrow \mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A^c) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A^c) = - \sum_{x:\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \gt \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)} |\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)| \] \[\Rightarrow (1 - \mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A)) - (1 - \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A)) = - \sum_{x:\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \gt \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)} |\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)| \] \[\Rightarrow \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A) = - \sum_{x:\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \gt \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)} |\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)| \] \[\Rightarrow \color{red}{|\mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A)| = \sum_{x:\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) \gt \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)} |\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)|} \tag{2}\] Now summing equation \(\color{red}{(1)}\) and equation \(\color{red}{(2)}\) we get,
\[\color{red}{2|\mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A)| = \sum_{x\in E} |\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)|}\] So \[\color{blue}{|\mathbb{P}_{\theta}(A) - \mathbb{P}_{\theta'}(A)| = \frac{1}{2}\sum_{x\in E} |\mathcal{p}_{\theta}(x) - \mathcal{p}_{\theta'}(x)|}\]
