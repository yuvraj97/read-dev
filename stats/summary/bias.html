If \(\hat{\theta}_n\) is an estimator of \(\theta\) then,
▶ Bias estimator \(\hat{\theta}_n\)
\[\text{bias}( \hat{\theta}_n ) = \mathbb{E}[ \hat{\theta}_n ] - \theta \] If \(\text{bias}=0 \Rightarrow \hat{\theta}_n\) is an unbiased estimator
▶ Quadratic Risk \(R\left( \hat{\theta}_n \right)\) of an estimator \(\hat{\theta}_n\)
\[ R \left( \hat{\theta}_n \right) = \mathbb{E} \left[ \left( \hat{\theta}_n - \theta \right)^2 \right] \] \[ R \left( \hat{\theta}_n \right) = Var\left(\hat{\theta}_n\right) + \text{bias}^2\left(\hat{\theta}_n\right) \]
