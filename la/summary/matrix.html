Vector interpretation of Matrix Space

Say we have a \(2\times 2\) matrix say \(A\in\mathbb{R}^{2\times 2}\)
\[A=\begin{bmatrix} \color{red}{\mathbb{R}} & \color{yellow}{\mathbb{R}} \\ \color{green}{\mathbb{R}} & \color{magenta}{\mathbb{R}} \\ \end{bmatrix} \] We can interpret it as vector say \(\vec{v}\in\mathbb{R}^4\) as,
\[\vec{v}=\begin{bmatrix} \color{red}{\mathbb{R}}\\ \color{yellow}{\mathbb{R}}\\ \color{green}{\mathbb{R}}\\ \color{magenta}{\mathbb{R}}\\ \end{bmatrix}\].
\(\bullet\) The vector space of \(\vec{v}\) is the Matrix Space of matrix \(A\)
